What is Terrorism?

Terrorism affects every corner of the globe, threatening peace and security. It involves violent acts intended to create fear, disrupt communities, and achieve political, ideological, or religious goals.

Future Trends in Terrorism

Advancing technologies are being leveraged by terrorist groups in increasingly sophisticated ways. Future trends include:

  • Cyber Terrorism: Targeting national infrastructures and databases.
  • Bioterrorism: Using biological agents to spread fear and disease.
  • Drone Warfare: Deploying unmanned vehicles for attacks.
